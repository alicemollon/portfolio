Hello! I’m Alice, a conceptual Illustrator and gif-maker, currently based in the South of France.

I create bright and bold illustrations for all sorts of projects - spanning digital products, editorial, publishing, animation, and more. I love to find succinct but characterful ways to communicate all kinds of ideas, making the niche and complex more accessible, or bringing a playful twist to everyday themes.
